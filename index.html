Event Instructions

Everything you need to know to participate in today's treasure hunt

Form Your Team

Teams of 3-5 people. You'll receive a unique team code from the organizers to start your hunt.

Access the App

Use any smartphone or tablet browser to access the treasure hunt app. No downloads required!

Scan QR Codes

Follow your assigned route and scan QR codes in the correct order. Each location has unique questions.

Take Team Photos

At each location, take a photo of your team with the QR code for verification by event organizers.

Time Limit

Complete as many locations as possible within the event time. First team to finish all locations wins!

Win Prizes

Prizes for fastest completion, most creative photos, and best teamwork. Everyone gets participation rewards!
